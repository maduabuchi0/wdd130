History

hite Water Rafting Co. began with a simple idea in the summer of 1998 — to share the thrill of river rafting with adventurers of all ages. What started as a small group of river guides with a passion for the outdoors has grown into a trusted name in adventure tourism. Over the past two decades, we’ve explored some of the most breathtaking rivers across the country, expanded our fleet of rafts, and welcomed thousands of guests to experience the power and beauty of nature. From our humble beginnings guiding weekend trips on local rapids, to leading full-day and multi-day expeditions on world-renowned rivers, our commitment to safety, excellence, and unforgettable memories has never changed. Today, we continue to uphold our founding mission: to deliver safe, fun, and inspiring adventures on the water..
